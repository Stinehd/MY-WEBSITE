PERSONAL PROJECTS

CULT OF THE LAMB - GOAT RIG

I wanted to learn rigging in Toonboom, so I made this rig of the Goat character from the game Cult of the Lamb by Massive Monster. I got the inspiration from this person, who made and shared their Lamb rig online: GUMROAD LINK and this official animation: YOUTUBE LINK

My goal was to make my own version and not look at how the lamb rig was build, untill the end to compare. So the rigs are completely different, but they still match in style.

My second goal was of course to make an animation with them together. I put my version of the cape on the Lamb rig for a closer match:
