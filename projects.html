EXAMPLES OF PROJECTS I HAVE BEEN A PART OF

JULIAN

From Cartoon saloons website: From the Academy Award® nominated director Louise Bagnall, comes a story of disco-very and celebration, inspired by the acclaimed childrens' book Julián is a Mermaid by Jessica Love.

I worked on clean-up in TVPaint.

From Cartoon saloons website: Julián is a co-production between Cartoon Saloon, Aircraft Pictures, Melusine Productions, Sun Creature and Wychwood Media.

THE SANDBOX

In this preschool show, we follow the small ladybug siblings Bror and Marie, on their wonderful daily adventures, in their sandbox.

I animated 11 episodes in Toonboom Harmony using rigs and additional 2D animation.

Created by Carl Quist-Møller and Katrin Quist-Møller, produced by Made by Us. Animation and compositing by JaFilm.

LITTLE TUT

Little Tut is a children's show for children aged 3 and under. Little Tut and her friends, Gato and Nisse, visit a new fantastic animal every day.

I animated 5 episodes in Toonboom Harmony using rigs and additional 2D animation.

Created by Maria Mac Dalland, produced by Morten Kaufmann and Toolbox Film, and co-produced and animated at Ja Film.

THE APESTAR

Jonna has lived at an orphanage all her life, but one day she gets adopted by a Gorilla. In this movie we follow Jonna as she gets used to her new life and her new mother.

I worked on both animation and clean-up

Directed by Linda Hambäck, produced by LEE Film in co-production with Mikrofilm, Nørlum, Film i Väst and Sveriges Television

BIG HERO 6: THE SERIES

Set after the events of the 3D movie, the series follows Hiro and his friends through school life and of course the fights they have with any villains who threaten San Fransokyo

I mainly worked on traditional 2D Animation and posing, but also clean-up, retakes and color and shadows in Toonboom Harmony.

I worked on the first season at Nørlum, who fully

produced 10 episodes for the series under the direction and in collaboration with Disney.

LEGO ELVES: WEBISODES

Emily Jones inherits her grandmother's magical amulet, that opens a portal so she can travel to elvendale, where she meets her 4 new elven friends.

Lego Elves webisodes: 7 - 31. I worked on animation, character turnarounds, posing and prop design.

The series is based on the Lego-elves lego sets. The episodes were produced by Jaflim and release on the official LEGO youtube channel
